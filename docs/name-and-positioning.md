# Name And Positioning

## Recommended Name

**VisaDesk AI**

## Why This Name Works

- Clear and practical.
- Professional enough for visa agents.
- Understandable to individual expats.
- Broad enough to expand from Thailand to Southeast Asia later.
- More trustworthy than playful names for a product handling passports, immigration forms, addresses, and signatures.

## Launch Positioning

**Thailand visa paperwork, filled from your saved profile.**

## Visa Agent Positioning

**Client intake, document checklists, and visa packets in minutes.**

## Other Name Options

- VisaDesk AI
- VisaDesk Thailand
- FormVisa
- VisaVault
- PermitPilot
- ExpatDesk
- VisaFlow
- StayReady
- EntryDesk
- PaperPass
- VisaPacket

## Recommendation

Use **VisaDesk AI** as the parent brand. If the first launch needs to feel highly local, use **VisaDesk Thailand** as the public-facing product name while keeping VisaDesk AI as the broader platform name.
