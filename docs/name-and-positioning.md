# Name And Positioning

## Recommended Name

**VisaFiler AI**

## Why This Name Works

- Clear and practical.
- Professional enough for visa agents.
- Understandable to individual expats.
- Broad enough to expand from Thailand to Southeast Asia later.
- More trustworthy than playful names for a product handling passports, immigration forms, addresses, and signatures.

## Launch Positioning

**Thailand visa paperwork, filled from your saved profile.**

## Visa Agent Positioning

**Client intake, document checklists, and visa packets in minutes.**

## Other Name Options

- FormVisa

- PermitPilot
- ExpatDesk

## Recommendation

Use **VisaFiler AI** as the parent brand on `visafilerai.com`. If the first launch needs to feel highly local, use **VisaFiler Thailand** as the public-facing product name while keeping VisaFiler AI as the broader platform name.
