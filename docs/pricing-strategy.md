# VisaFiler AI Pricing Strategy

## Recommendation

Use different pricing models for individuals and visa agents.

- **Individuals:** annual pass plus optional one-off packet pricing.
- **Visa agents:** monthly or annual SaaS subscription.

This matches usage behavior. Individual expats need help around renewal events, so monthly billing will create cancellation friction. Agents use the product operationally, so subscription pricing makes sense.

## Individual Expat Pricing

### Free Trial

Let users create a profile, complete a checklist, and preview a watermarked PDF for free.

The free trial should prove the magic moment without giving away the final value. A user should see that the app filled the form correctly, but exporting a clean PDF should require payment.

### One-Off Packet

Suggested price: **THB 299 to THB 499 per completed packet**.

This is useful for tourists or one-time users who do not want a subscription.

### 12-Month Expat Pass

Suggested price: **THB 990 to THB 1,490 per year**.

Recommended starting point: **THB 1,290 per year**.

Included:

- Profile vault.
- Supported form generation.
- Document checklist.
- Renewal reminders.
- Print-ready PDF exports.
- Limited packet allowance, such as 3 to 5 packets per year.

### Couple / Family Pass

Suggested price: **THB 1,990 per year**.

This is relevant for couples, spouses, families, and retirees where paperwork is often repeated across more than one person.

## Visa Agent Pricing

Agents should be priced around time saved, client volume, and intake organization.

### Starter

Suggested price: **THB 1,990 per month**.

For small agents or solo operators.

### Pro

Suggested price: **THB 4,990 per month**.

For busier agents who process regular monthly client volume.

### Agency

Suggested price: **THB 9,900+ per month**.

For teams that need staff accounts, branded intake links, higher client limits, and larger packet volume.

### Recommended Early Offer

Start agent pilots at:

**THB 2,990 per month or THB 29,900 per year.**

Offer annual billing with two months free.

## Pricing Tests

Run these early tests before locking the model:

- One-off packet vs 12-month pass for individual expats.
- Retirement self-filing packet at THB 990 to THB 1,490, anchored against agent packages commonly quoted at 40,000 to 60,000 THB.
- Watermarked preview vs limited free clean export.
- Agent pricing by active clients vs generated packets.
- Whether agents value branded intake links enough to pay for a higher plan.
- Whether couples/families convert better than single-user plans.

## Positioning

Do not position the individual plan as cheaper software. Position it as avoiding stress, mistakes, and repeated form work.

Recommended message:

**Generate print-ready Thailand visa paperwork from your saved profile in minutes.**

For agents:

**Collect client details once, track missing documents, and generate consistent immigration packets faster.**
