# Go-To-Market Notes

## Recommended First Market

Start with **Thailand expat visa admin** and make the product useful for both individual expats and visa agents.

The strongest first commercial target is likely **visa agents**, because the product saves staff time every week and helps them process more clients with fewer missing-document delays.

## Individual Expat Channels

- Facebook expat groups.
- Thailand visa and travel groups.
- Reddit communities.
- YouTube/TikTok demos.
- SEO guides for TM.7, TM.30, 90-day reports, and visa extensions.
- Condo communities and local expat businesses.
- Free downloadable TM.7 checklist as a lead magnet.

## Visa Agent Channels

- Direct outreach to Thai visa agents.
- Short demo video showing a TM.7 packet generated from a saved profile.
- Free pilot for 5 to 10 client packets.
- Offer branded client intake links as a higher-plan feature.
- Emphasize saved staff time, fewer missing documents, and consistent packet output.

## Core Messaging

For expats:

**Stop retyping the same visa paperwork. Generate print-ready Thailand visa forms from your saved profile.**

For agents:

**Collect client details once, track missing documents, and generate Thailand visa packets in minutes.**

## Positioning Warning

Do not lead with "generic AI form filler." That market is broad, crowded, and less trusted. Lead with a specific outcome:

**Thailand visa paperwork made simple.**
