# Business Evaluation

## Short Verdict

The generic idea of "AI fills forms" is not strong enough by itself. The stronger business is a niche product for Thailand expat visa admin and visa agents.

## Ratings

- Generic AI form filler: **4/10**
- AI PDF filler for any niche: **5/10**
- Thailand expat visa/admin assistant: **7/10**
- B2B tool for visa agents/relocation firms: **8/10**
- Healthcare prior-auth assistant: **8/10 market, 4/10 ease of execution**

## Why Thailand Visa Admin Is Attractive

- Clear recurring pain.
- Users already pay visa agents to avoid admin work.
- Forms are repetitive and often still paper/PDF based.
- Expats are easy to reach through social media groups and local communities.
- Visa agents have higher willingness to pay because the product saves staff time.
- Thailand is narrow enough to build well before expanding.

## Main Risks

- Trust and accuracy matter because the app handles immigration paperwork.
- Passport, address, visa, signature, and document data require strong security.
- Immigration forms and local requirements can change.
- Some steps still require printing, photos, wet signatures, and in-person submission.
- The product must avoid being perceived as legal advice.

## Strategic Recommendation

Build the app so individual expats can use it, but sell the first paid B2B version to visa agents.

First wedge:

- TM.7 extension form.
- Profile vault.
- Missing-info checklist.
- PDF preview.
- Print-ready export.
- Agent dashboard after the individual workflow works.

Expansion:

- Add more Thai immigration forms.
- Add agent intake links and packet tracking.
- Expand to Southeast Asia only after Thailand is working well.
